Who am I?

Hi, I’m Guillermo Hernandez. I'm 22 years old and I live in Mexico City. Right now I'm studying computer engineering at Facultad de Ingenieria of UNAM. Although I don't work formally, I'm always doing some extra work from school. Sometimes personal projects or help my father in his lab (he's a teacher at Facultad de Quimica). Since high school, I knew i wanted to study engineering, but didn't know what. During the pandemic, I started my research and found that programming was my path.

In the future, I would like to work doing sports analytics or data engineer for a sports team. I’m really passionate about sports. I never practiced them regularly, but have always liked them. During my life, I practiced taekwondo, soccer, and swimming, but I never got caught up enough to continue. My love for sports comes from my father, who likes to watch soccer, football, baseball, and hockey.

I also enjoy traveling a lot; my most recent trip was to Buenos Aires, and I thoroughly loved it. The part that I liked the most was eating beef every day at a good price. Also, the wine was incredible. I don't know much about wine, but we went to many wine tastings. The culture was interesting, but the soccer passion was incredible.
